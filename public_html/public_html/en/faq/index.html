1. What is Petalytix?

Petalytix is a Google Maps review data analysis service that helps businesses understand customers, improve reputation, and make data-driven strategic decisions.

2. Where does the review data come from?

Review data is collected from Google Maps using legal and safe automated web scraping technology, then processed into easy-to-understand insights.

3. What types of businesses can use Petalytix?

Petalytix is suitable for any business with a location on Google Maps, including restaurants, cafes, hotels, salons, retail stores, and service providers.

4. What are the main services offered?

We offer four main services: Customer Sentiment & Review Analysis, Business Reputation & Rating Monitoring, Advanced Analysis (pricing, peak hours, competitor comparison), and Strategy & Service Improvement Recommendations.

5. How often is the data updated?

Update frequency depends on the selected package—ranging from a one-time purchase without regular updates to monthly progress monitoring.

6. Can I compare my business with competitors?

Yes. Petalytix’s benchmarking feature allows you to see your reputation, pricing, and performance compared to competitors in the same area.

7. Do I need technical skills to use Petalytix?

No. Our reports and dashboards are designed to be user-friendly, with no advanced data analysis skills required.

8. Does Petalytix provide custom reports tailored to my business?

Yes. We can create custom reports based on the metrics and format you need.

9. How do I get started with Petalytix?

You can register directly on this page and see for yourself how the platform works. If you are interested, you can contact us at contact@petalytix.id or the WhatsApp number listed on the Contact Us page. After making the payment via transfer and once it is confirmed by our team, you will receive the service you purchased within 24 hours.

10. Does Petalytix offer a demo before purchase?

Yes. We provide a free demo to show how the platform works and the insights you can gain.
