Smart Analysis Services for Everyone

We provide data analysis solutions that are accessible, accurate, and relevant for all types of businesses—from SMEs to large corporations. Using intelligent analytics and high-value data sources, we transform reviews and ratings into actionable business insights that help improve strategy, service, and reputation. No deep technical expertise is required—anyone can leverage data to make smarter, more impactful decisions.

Business Reputation & Rating Monitoring

Business Reputation & Rating Analysis Services are designed to help you understand and manage your business image on online platforms, especially Google Maps. We regularly collect and update review and rating data and perform aggregate analyses to provide an accurate big-picture view of your business performance from the customers’ perspective. Some of the analyses you will receive include:

  • Overall Rating — see how your business is perceived across all branches/outlets and track trends over time.
  • Price Rating — understand customer perceptions of your pricing.
  • Menu Rating — identify the most-reviewed items or dishes with the highest (or lowest) ratings.
  • Other Aspect Ratings — map customer ratings across multiple dimensions, such as food quality, ambience, service, waiting time, noise, and parking.
  • Root Cause Analysis — evaluate the performance of each branch/outlet and identify locations that require further improvement.


This approach ensures you maintain full control over public perception, enabling quick actions to address issues while leveraging positive reviews to boost customer loyalty and attract new clients.

 
 

Sentiment & Customer Review Analysis

Customer Sentiment & Review Analysis Services help businesses gain deep insights from every comment left by customers on Google Maps. We don’t just count star ratings—we also process review text using Natural Language Processing (NLP) techniques to understand sentiment (positive, negative, or neutral) and assess the relevance of each review. Some of the analyses you will receive include:

  • Word Cloud — identify the words or topics most frequently mentioned by customers.
  • Sentiment Analysis — understand customer appreciation from positive sentiments and gather actionable feedback or criticism from negative sentiments, ranked by relevance and factual accuracy.


This approach enables businesses to make decisions based on factual data and avoid biases. The result is more targeted service improvement strategies, a stronger reputation, and a consistently better customer experience.

Advanced Analysis

Advanced Analysis Services provides a deeper strategic perspective by leveraging multiple data dimensions from Google Maps. Going beyond reviews and ratings, this analysis explores market reach within a specific radius, per-person pricing perceptions, peak visit times, and your business reputation compared to competitors in the same category and area. Some of the analyses you will receive include:

  • Market Reach Analysis — understand how far each branch/outlet’s market extends.
  • Price per Person Analysis — learn about customers’ spending potential across different locations.
  • Operational Timing Analysis — identify peak days and hours to optimise staff allocation, promotions, or inventory.
  • Menu/Service Analysis — discover customers’ favourite items or services over time.
  • Benchmarking — see your business position on the competitive map, including ratings, number of reviews, and other aspects.
  • Custom Page — create visualisations of business metrics tailored to your needs.


This approach helps businesses design strategies that are not only reactive to issues but also proactive in seizing growth opportunities often hidden within the data.

 
 

Strategy Recommendations & Service Improvement

Service Improvement & Strategy Recommendation Services are designed to turn data and analysis into actionable steps that businesses can implement immediately. Based on insights from customer reviews, sentiment trends, competitor comparisons, and operational data, we provide recommendations that are specific, measurable, and relevant to your business context.

With this service, you will receive:

  • One-page Report — a concise summary of your business image performance down to specific locations or aspects.
  • Detailed Report — an in-depth view of your business image performance, including all locations and key aspects.
  • Improvement Recommendations — strategic action steps to address locations or aspects that have a significant impact on ratings and customer reviews.


This service helps users quickly understand analysis results and supports making strategic business decisions.
