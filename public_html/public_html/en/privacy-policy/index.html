Privacy Policy

Petalytix is committed to protecting the privacy and personal data of our service users. This document explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

1. Information We Collect
We may collect:

  • Account information such as name, email, phone number, and payment details from users.
  • Operational data is necessary to provide our services, such as report preferences or dashboard configurations.
  • Public data available on Google Maps, including reviews, ratings, photos, and business information (we do not collect personal information).

2. How We Use Information
The collected information is used to:

  • Provide analysis services and reports according to your needs.
  • Develop, improve, and customise Petalytix features.
  • Provide customer support and send service-related information.
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations.

3. Public Data from Google Maps
Petalytix only processes public data available on Google Maps and does not access or modify information protected by personal accounts. This data is used solely for analysis purposes.

4. Data Protection
We implement technical and administrative security measures to protect data from unauthorised access, alteration, or disclosure.

5. Sharing Information with Third Parties
We do not sell or share personal data with third parties for commercial purposes. Data is shared only when necessary to:

  • Comply with applicable laws and regulations.
  • Provide services through trusted partners (e.g., server providers or payment services).

6. Data Storage & Retention
Data will be stored as long as needed to fulfil the collection purpose or as required by law. Users can request data deletion at any time via contact@petalytix.id.

7. User Rights
You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold.
  • Request updates, corrections, or deletion of personal data.
  • Withdraw consent for future data use.

8. Changes to the Privacy Policy
This policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be announced on our website.

9. Contact Us
If you have questions regarding this privacy policy, please contact us at:
📧 contact@petalytix.id
