Driving Strategic Business Growth

Google Maps has now become the largest source of online reviews, accounting for almost 75% of consumer opinions on the internet (ReputationStacker, 2024). On the other hand, an increase of just one star in ratings can boost visit conversions by up to 44% (SoCi, 2022). Business owners can seize this golden opportunity by making more informed decisions through the analysis of ratings, customer reviews, and visitation patterns—efforts that can enhance their reputation and strengthen market visibility.
