# AyaKasir Pricing Scheme
## Series: Pertumbuhan & Skala

## GOAL
Design a freemium pricing model optimized for Indonesian UMKM.
Drive user acquisition through free plan and monetize via upgrades to Tumbuh and Mapan.

---

## PACKAGES

### 1. PERINTIS (FREE)
Target:
- New businesses
- Warung, kios, small merchants

Price:
- Free

Core Value:
- Simple transaction recording

Features:
- 1 owner + 1 cashier
- Products, Customers, Menu up to 100 items each
- Add manual raw materials, customers, menu product
- Up to 1000 transactions per month
- Payment methods: Tunai, QRIS, Transfer, Utang
- Access to web and android app
- Basic end of day report
- Connect to printer / digital receipt
- Basic cloud backup (auto-sync)

Limitations:
- No advanced reports
- No multi-branch
- No CSV export (Unduh Data disabled — requires Tumbuh/Mapan)
- Limited staff management

Upgrade Triggers:
- Product limit reached (>100)
- Need multiple staffs
- Need better reporting

CTA:
- "Mulai Gratis"

---

### 2. TUMBUH (PRO)
Target:
- Growing UMKM
- Small shops, cafes, minimarkets

Price:
- Rp29,900 / month (strikethrough from Rp49,900 — 40% off promo) --> Currently Free 3 Months!

Core Value:
- Operational control and efficiency

Features:
- 1 Owner and 2 Staffs in each branch
- Products, Customers, Menu up to 300 items each
- Bulk upload for raw materials, customers, menu product
- Up to 6.000 transactions per month
- Max. 3 branches (Owner Office multi-branch dashboard available)
- Payment methods: Tunai, QRIS, Transfer, and Utang
- Access to web and android app + Owner Office page (consolidated reports, inventory analytics, master data linking)
- Advanced reports and raw data access
- Connect to printer / digital receipt
- Real-time cloud sync

Limitations:
- No advanced analytics
- No API access

Upgrade Triggers:
- Multiple store locations
- Need deeper insights
- Business scaling

CTA:
- "Upgrade Sekarang"

Badge:
- "Paling Populer"

---

### 3. MAPAN (ENTERPRISE)
Target:
- Established businesses
- Multi-branch stores, restaurants, franchises

Price:
- Rp19,900 / branch / month (strikethrough from Rp59,900 — 67% off founding price)

Core Value:
- Full business control at scale

Features:
- All Tumbuh features, no limits (unlimited branches, staff, products, customers, transactions)
- Owner Office with unlimited branches + advanced inventory analytics
- 1 feature request / year (conditional on technical feasibility)
- Payment Gateway (Coming Soon)
- Priority support
- Onboarding & training
- Setup by developer team

CTA:
- "Hubungi Developer" (links to WhatsApp)
